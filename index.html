About UDHA

Uganda Development and Health Associates (UDHA) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving health outcomes, advancing human rights, and fostering sustainable development within Ugandan communities.

Our various initiatives include supporting individuals with TB, HIV, and AIDS; female empowerment through vocational training; nutrition education for children and mothers; youth sexual health education; community health centers; and literacy training. We collaborate with healthcare professionals, other nonprofit organizations, and community leaders to create lasting impact.
