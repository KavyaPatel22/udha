OUR HISTORY

Uganda Development and Health Associates (UDHA) was founded in April of 2003 in the Iganga district, Busoga Region, Eastern Uganda. Since then, the organization has been dedicated to enhancing and expanding healthcare for underserved communities by empowering youth, women, and children. Using sustainable mechanisms and drawing upon a foundation of expertise, UDHA strives to help communities meet their needs.

"A society fully empowered to participate and take care of their own health and developmental needs."
